Food Distribution

Clients receive 60 - 80 pounds of food every week. Food includes meat, dairy, fresh fruit, vegetables, and items with a long shelf life. Eastmont Community Center is committed to providing its clients with programs and resources that work for them.

Our Impact in 2023

3,300 Households Served

15,125 Individuals Fed

490,000 Pounds of Food Distributed

How Can I Participate?

1. Meet the Requirements

  • You need to live within the 1st Supervisorial District of Los Angeles County.
  • Electric/gas bills, and pay-stubs/SSI statements to verify income and location of your residence.
  • ID is preferred but not required.
  • Unhoused folks are welcomed and are not screened for service area requirements.

2. Complete Registration

  • An appointment is required to determine your eligibility and register to the program.
